    How to play

  • There is a girl who have to collect 3 golds for winning the game.
  • The girl will have to climb the ladder and collect the gold.
  • Here Ladders are not having the planks so your task is to put the planks in the ladder for climbing.
  • Consider the ladder as a data structure Stack and push and pop planks accordingly.
  • Push the plank into the ladder,to climb the ladder and collect the gold.
  • There are 6 planks and 3 ladders for collecting 3 gold.
  • First step is to push the planks in ladder1.
  • Second step is to pop the planks from ladder1,push them into ladder2.
  • Third step is to pop the planks from ladder2,push them into ladder3.
  • After every step climb the ladder and gold will be collected.
  • You have three lifes to win the game.
  • For every wrong push/pop life will be reduced.
